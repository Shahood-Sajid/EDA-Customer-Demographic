 2   first_name                           
 3   last_name                          
 4   gender                               
 5   past_3_years_bike_related_purchases   
 6   DOB                                  
 7   job_title                             
 8   job_industry_category                 
 9   wealth_segment                       
 10  deceased_indicator                   
 11  owns_car                             
 12  tenure
